africantea

description

A warmly made tea from refined tea leaves from Africa sprinkled with fresh cinnamon leaves

Ingredients

  • 2 liters of cow milk
  • 4 table spoon of sugar
  • 2 table spoon of tea leaves prefambly KETEPA TEA
  • 2 leaves of fressh cinnamon leaves

steps

  1. On a sauce pan,bring milk to a boil preferebaly 100.5°C (212.9°F)
  2. Concurrently as boils pour the tea leaves inside the sauce pan
  3. Boiled,pour your sugar and stir
  4. Add cinnamon leaves for the authetic and rich deep taste to serve
